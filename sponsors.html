Arcis's engineering work is funded and supported by sponsors who believe in student-driven aerospace engineering. Their contributions directly enable our design, fabrication, and competition activities.

Why sponsor
Team Arcis?

Arcis is not a casual college project. The team functions as a real engineering organisation — with technical deliverables, competition deadlines, and a multi-year track record. Sponsoring Arcis means backing a team that produces demonstrable engineering work and competes at an international level.

Sponsors gain direct visibility among technically focused engineering students — the future workforce of aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing industries. Many of our alumni have joined engineering organisations in these sectors.

Beyond visibility, sponsors who engage actively with the team have the opportunity to shape real engineering problems, offer mentorship, and identify talent before graduation.

Talent Pipeline

Direct access to technically motivated engineering students across aerodynamics, structures, avionics, and manufacturing. Arcis alumni regularly enter engineering careers.

Year-Round Visibility

Sponsor logos appear on the aircraft, team apparel, this website, competition documentation, and all official team communications throughout the season.

Competition Association

Association with SAE Aero Design and AIAA DBF — internationally recognised competitions that attract technical judges and institutions from across the world.
