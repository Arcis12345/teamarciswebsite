Arcis's engineering work is funded and supported by the college and sponsors who believe in student-driven aerospace engineering. Their contributions directly enable our design, fabrication, and competition activities.

Why sponsor
Team Arcis?

Sponsoring Arcis means backing a team that produces demonstrable engineering work and competes at an international level. The team functions as an engineering organisation, with technical deliverables, competition deadlines, and a multi-year track record.

Sponsors gain international visibility among technically focused engineering students who are the future workforce of aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing industries.

Student & Talent Reach

Connect with over 200+ engineering students annually through workshops, technical events, and team activities, creating direct visibility among aerospace-focused talent.

Brand Visibility

Sponsor branding is featured on aircraft liveries, team apparel, banners, and digital platforms throughout the season.

Digital Promotion

Gain exposure through sponsor centric reels, build updates, competition coverage, and short-form content shared throughout the year.

Community & Industry Engagement

Participate in workshops, technical sessions, and collaborative events that strengthen connections with students and the broader engineering community.
