Vayu

Latest SAE Aero Design East Regular Class

The primary objective of this year is to design, manufacture, and fly a multi-engine cargo aircraft powered by a single 4-cell LiPo battery that maximizes payload capacity and safely enclosing standard 2-liter plastic bottles.
Vayu is a twin-motor high lift aircraft with a payload fraction of 0.61.

Athena

Completed AIAA DBF 2025 21st Globally Technical Design Report

The primary objective of this year is inspired by the Bell X-1 supersonic flight program and was to design, build, and fly an RC primary carrier aircraft capable of carrying heavy wing-pylon-mounted structural payloads and successfully deploying an autonomous, glider mid-flight to land precisely within a designated bonus zone.

Avalon

Completed AIAA DBF 2025

The 2024 AIAA DBF was themed around Urban Air Mobility (UAM). The objective was to design, manufacture, and fly an RC aircraft configured to fit a strict 2.5-foot parking space and execute a 20-foot short takeoff while carrying combinations of crew, flight passengers, and medical transport payload.
